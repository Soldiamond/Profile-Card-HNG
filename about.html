My Journey

My name is Solution Abiola Owoche, and yes - Solution is my real name! I've always been fascinated by the intersection of creativity and technology. As a junior web developer and creative graphic designer, I believe in building solutions that are not just functional but also beautiful and intuitive.

My journey into tech started with graphic design, where I learned the importance of visual storytelling. This naturally evolved into web development as I wanted to bring my designs to life. When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me playing my acoustic guitar, getting lost in anime worlds, or simply enjoying the comfort of my home.

My Goals in This Program

Through the HNG Internship, I aim to:

  • Specialize in creating accessible and user-friendly web experiences
